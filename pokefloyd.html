Pokémon Floyd Version

This is the Beta Version of Pokémon Floyd, and as such more badges and content are to be added!

Controls are as follows:

A - "z"

B - "x"

Up - "Arrow Up"

Down - "Arrow Down"

Left - "Arrow Left"

Right - "Arrow Right"

Start - "Enter"

Select - "Space"

To play this game, please, download the latest Flash player!

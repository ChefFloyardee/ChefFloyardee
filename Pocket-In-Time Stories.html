Pocket-In-Time Stories

Throughout my life, and likely your life as well, you've experienced days in which strange, unusual events happen that strayed quite far from the norm. I call these days, when something quite bizarre, unexpected, or just flat out memorable for being so out of your ordinary routine, "Pocket-In-Time Days" and they thus make for great stories since they've happened to me quite often! Note though that the time that a hot air balloon landed into my backyard when I was a kid will not be included here, but not due to my not having suitable pictures! This is instead due to the fact that this has now since happened to me several more times!
